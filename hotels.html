What Makes a Hotel "Muslim-Friendly"?

When booking, look for these amenities: halal food (or at least vegetarian/seafood options and halal breakfast on request), prayer facilities (prayer mats, qibla direction, or a quiet prayer space), alcohol-free mini-bars where possible, and proximity to mosques and halal restaurants. Most hotels are happy to accommodate requests if you contact them in advance.

Tip: always message the hotel before arrival to confirm halal breakfast or prayer mat availability, as these are often "on request" rather than standard.
