Halal Food Near Sagrada Familia

The best halal restaurants within walking distance of Barcelona's most famous landmark — plus where to stay nearby.

← Back to home

The Sagrada Familia is Barcelona's most-visited landmark, drawing millions of tourists every year. The good news for Muslim travelers: the surrounding Eixample neighborhood has plenty of excellent halal restaurants within easy walking distance, from Pakistani and Indian to Turkish and Lebanese.

Quick tip: Most halal spots are a 5–15 minute walk from the basilica, concentrated around Carrer de Mallorca and the wider Eixample grid. After visiting Sagrada Familia, walk a few blocks for a great-value halal meal.

Best Halal Restaurants Near Sagrada Familia

✓ Halal

One of the highest-rated halal restaurants in the Sagrada Familia area. Authentic Nepali and Indian dishes with vegan and vegetarian options available. A local favorite for good reason.

✓ Halal

Highly rated for flavorful curries, tandoori, and biryani. A reliable, welcoming spot just a short walk from the basilica — great for groups and families.

Visiting Sagrada Familia — Practical Tips

Book tickets in advance. The basilica is extremely popular and timed-entry tickets sell out, especially in summer. Go early or late to avoid the biggest crowds. Combine your visit with lunch at one of the halal spots above — most are a short, pleasant walk through the Eixample grid. Getting there: the Sagrada Familia metro station (L2 purple, L5 blue) drops you right at the entrance.

With world-class architecture above and great halal food just around the corner, the Sagrada Familia area is a perfect base for Muslim travelers exploring Barcelona.
